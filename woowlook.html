Woow-Look (Sallon App)

Woowlook is a pioneering project revolutionizing the personal grooming and salon services industry in the UK. This comprehensive platform not only facilitates easy online shopping for a variety of grooming products but also connects users with skilled salon service providers, creating a one-stop solution for all grooming needs.

User-Centric Account Management

Woowlook prioritizes user experience, offering a user-friendly account management system where customers can seamlessly navigate between product purchases and salon service bookings.

Diverse Seller Network

The platform boasts a network of skilled sellers providing a range of salon services, including hair cutting, shaving, and more. These sellers are vetted for their expertise and commitment to quality service.

Effortless Booking Process

Users can easily browse through a list of reputable sellers, view their services, and book appointments directly through the Woowlook platform. The streamlined booking process ensures a hassle-free experience.

Mobile App and Website

Woowlook provides a seamless experience across devices with a dedicated mobile app and a responsive website. Users can effortlessly manage their grooming routines, purchase products, and schedule salon services from anywhere.

Admin Section

The admin section of Woowlook empowers administrators to oversee seller performance, manage bookings, and ensure a high standard of service quality. This ensures a reliable and consistent experience for users.
