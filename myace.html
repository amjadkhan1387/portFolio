MyAce (Online Booking for Food and Rides)

MyAce is a revolutionary online platform that seamlessly integrates two essential services - food and ride bookings. With a user-friendly interface, MyAce provides customers with the convenience of accessing a diverse range of restaurants and stores offering an array of delectable foods, products, and addons. Simultaneously, the platform features a ride-sharing module that connects users with nearby drivers, creating a comprehensive and efficient solution for both culinary delights and transportation needs.

Diverse Culinary Experience

MyAce brings together a myriad of restaurants and stores, each offering a unique menu of foods, products, and addons. Customers can explore a variety of options to cater to their specific cravings and preferences.

Effortless Food Ordering

Customers can effortlessly browse through the platform, exploring menus, and placing orders for their favorite foods. From delightful cuisines to special addons, MyAce ensures a seamless food ordering experience.

Comprehensive Store Listings

The platform showcases a diverse array of stores, each presenting an exclusive collection of products and services. From culinary delights to retail therapy, customers can explore a multitude of options within a single platform.

Ride-Sharing Module

MyAce goes beyond culinary experiences by offering a robust ride-sharing module. Drivers can create profiles, providing details of their documents, licenses, and vehicles, ensuring a secure and trustworthy environment for users.

Driver Search and Booking

Customers seeking reliable transportation can easily locate nearby drivers through the platform. The integrated search functionality enables users to find available vehicles, view driver profiles, and make appointments with their preferred choices.

Real-Time Tracking

The platform incorporates real-time tracking features for both food deliveries and rides. Users can track the status of their orders and the location of their booked rides, enhancing transparency and convenience.
